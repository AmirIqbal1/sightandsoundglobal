Foundational Documents

Our Vision
Sight and Sound Global’s objective is to relieve children with vision or hearing difficulties by
raising awareness on social media, fundraising, and paying for necessary medical equipment,
procedures, and special education to make their life easier.


While a public health service helps many people and does a great job at care overall for many
different walks of life, there is still room for professional and personal care to be given. It is known
that the U.K. can be overwhelmed and have long times, especially with treatments.


We would relieve the burden and help parents alike; we will be able to provide doctors and
professional opinions to speed & aid with specific care as well as provide vital information and
equipment to those that need it.



Our Mission
We At Sight and Sound Global do this by an assessment of needs for care and support is carried out,
for deaf or blind children. We do this with a team with specific training and expertise in our
purpose. In particular, we assess the need for communication, one-to-one human contact, social
interaction, emotional well-being, and support with mobility assistive technology.


We provide appropriate services to deaf/blind children, recognizing that they may not necessarily be
able to benefit from mainstream services or those services aimed primarily at blind or deaf people
who can rely on their other senses. The impact of dual sensory impairment on an individual will
vary according to their learning opportunities.


Children born Deaf/ Blind may have little or no formal language and only limited understanding of
the world because they have never been able to watch/listen to other people and the things that are
happening around them.



Our Core Values
Over 1 million disabled children in the U.K. could benefit from our charity.
We have an open policy and keep everything legal. We even use third parties to handle cash/ card
donations and funding as an extra layer of protection.


The Sight and Sound Global are a dedicated team that helps those less fortunate or speeding up
assistance with everyday living.

A big part of the team is volunteers who devote our time, money,
and energy to help the younger generation.
