About Us

Sight and Sound Global love working with others and thriving while helping a fantastic cause.


That is why our team is so close-knit, we all believe in a goal that is great and will benefit the children of the UK.

Sight and Sound started with three very passionate siblings, who wanted to change lives. Starting initially in 2020 and then becoming a full-time and registered charity in 2022, we have come a long way in getting officially recognised by the Charity Commission.

We intend on helping and volunteering to the best of our abilities, all while having a smile on our faces.

Amir, Amina and Faheem Iqbal. 

Vision

To help children of the UK, with Sight or Sound disabilities.

Mission 

We will raise funds, raise awareness, create goals and work with other organisations to fulfil our goals.

Values

We undergo background checks and have policies in place to protect others. We operate on the highest efficiency, all whilst cutting costs to maximize donations and help with acquiring equipment for children.

Make a Difference Today.

There are around 1 million disabled children in the U.K. Thousands of them rely on people like us, to get the equipment and the health services they need.
